Yeh Jawaani Hai Deewani

Yeh Jawaani Hai Deewani is a 2013 Bollywood coming-of-age romance film that explores themes of friendship, relationships, and romance. The movie stars Ranbir Kapoor as Kabir Thapar, also known as Bunny, and Deepika Padukone as Naina Talwar, a medical student.The story follows the two characters as they bond during a trekking trip, but Kabir leaves India for his career before Naina can express herself. Years later, they meet again, but Kabir's dreams still come before their relationship. The movie also explores different friendship dynamics through the characters of Bunny and Aditi, and Avi and Bunny

chhichhore

Chhichhore is a 2019 Bollywood movie about a group of engineering students who go from being losers to choosers. The movie is told through the lens of Anirudh (Sushant Singh Rajput), a middle-aged man who reminisces about his college days with his friends after a tragic incident. Anirudh believes that sharing stories from his college years can help his son, Raghav (who is played by a child actor), recover mentally after attempting suicide. The movie is a mix of Student Of The Year, 3 Idiots, and other campus films, and it deals with serious issues like student suicide and communication.
