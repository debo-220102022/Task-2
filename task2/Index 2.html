Maidaan

Maidaan is a 2024 Hindi-language biographical sports drama film about the life of Syed Abdul Rahim, a pioneering football coach and manager in India during the 1950s and 1960s.

chhichhore

Chhichhore is a 2019 Bollywood movie about a group of engineering students who go from being losers to choosers. The movie is told through the lens of Anirudh (Sushant Singh Rajput), a middle-aged man who reminisces about his college days with his friends after a tragic incident. Anirudh believes that sharing stories from his college years can help his son, Raghav (who is played by a child actor), recover mentally after attempting suicide. The movie is a mix of Student Of The Year, 3 Idiots, and other campus films, and it deals with serious issues like student suicide and communication.

Badhaai Do

Badhaai Do is a 2022 Bollywood comedy-drama film about a gay man and a lesbian woman who enter into a marriage of convenience to appease their families' pressure to get married.

Junglee

The film is about a veterinarian named Raj Nair (Vidyut Jammwal) who returns to his father's elephant reserve in Odisha after ten years and faces off against an international poaching ring. The film also features Pooja Sawant, Asha Bhat, Atul Kulkarni, Akshay Oberoi, and elephants Didi and Bhola. It was released on March 29, 2019 and received praise for its cinematography and action sequences.

Crew

Crew is a 2024 Indian Hindi-language heist comedy film directed by Rajesh A Krishnan and written by Nidhi Mehra and Mehul Suri. Produced by Ekta Kapoor, Rhea Kapoor, Anil Kapoor, and Digvijay Purohit under Balaji Motion Pictures and Anil Kapoor Films & Communication Network, it stars Tabu, Kareena Kapoor Khan and Kriti Sanon with Diljit Dosanjh and Kapil Sharma in supporting roles. In the film, three air hostesses become involved in a gold smuggling operation.

Raazi

Raazi is a 2018 Hindi-language mystery and thriller film about a young Indian woman who becomes a spy in Pakistan during the 1971 Indo-Pakistani War.
